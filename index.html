Frequently Asked Questions

What are your check-in and check-out timings?

Our check-in time is 10:00 AM and check-out is 6:00 PM. Early or late slots can be arranged on request.

What vaccinations are required before a stay?

Dogs must be vaccinated for rabies, distemper, and parvo. Please carry proof of vaccination during check-in.

Can I bring my pet’s favorite toy or blanket?

Absolutely! Familiar items help reduce stress and make your pet feel at home.
